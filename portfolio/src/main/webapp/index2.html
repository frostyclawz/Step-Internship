Favorite Games!

As an avid gamer, I thought that I would make a list of my favorite games to play and/or have completed!

  • Destiny
  • Smite
  • Spyro: Reignited Trilogy


Destiny

Destiny, as a franchise, has become one of my favorite games to play over the nearly six years that the franchise has been around. With this, the action-FPS MMO-lite science fantasy game proves to draw me in with its captivating lore and theorizing potential. While the current content for the game Destiny 2 has been lacking overall... I will continue to play the major franchise that I have come to love.


My Guardian Class?: Warlock



Smite

Smite is one of the games that I enjoy to play every so often. Whether it be when I'm bored or when I am not playing Destiny, Smite is usually a mostly enjoyable experience. Smite is a MOBA played in the third-person perspective rather than the usual bird's-eye view. It plays pactically like your normal MOBA like League of Legends or DOTA 2 with very similar mechanics. All of the characters you play as are gods and goddesses of pantheons long gone... (or various monsters and literary gods). Despite the frustration with balancing, the game proves to be a fun game to sit down and play from time to time.


My main in Smite?: Kukulkan



Spyro: Reignited Trilogy

Spyro is one of those franchises I've been interested in without playing the main games of the franchise. Maybe just because of my love for dragons! Having the original three games remastered for modern consoles was an awesome thing to see and immediately garnered a preorder from me. Being able to finally experience the original trilogy of games in an updated fashion was an amazing experience. I've always had a fondness of older gamees, and getting to play a remake of of these games was simply... awesome.


Favorite Spyro Game?: Spyro 2
